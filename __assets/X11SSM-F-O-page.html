                      Key Features


                      1. Single socket H4 (LGA 1151) supports Intel® Xeon® processor E3-1200 v6/v5, Intel® 7th/6th Gen. Core™ i3 series,
                        Intel® Celeron® and Intel® Pentium®
                      2. Intel® C236 chipset
                      3. Up to 64GB Unbuffered ECC UDIMM DDR4 2400MHz; 4 DIMM slots
                      4. Expansion slots:
                        1 PCIe 3.0 x8 (in x16),
                        1 PCIe 3.0 x8,
                        2 PCIe 3.0 x4 (in x8)
                      5. Dual GbE LAN with Intel® i210-AT
                      6. 8 SATA3 (6Gbps) via C236;
                        RAID 0, 1, 5, 10
                      7. Integrated IPMI 2.0 and KVM with Dedicated LAN
                      8. 5 USB 3.0 (2 rear, 2 via header(s), 1 Type A), 6 USB 2.0 (2 rear, 4 via header(s))
                      9. I/O: 1 VGA, 2 COM, 2 SuperDOM, and TPM header
